Privacy Policy

Meditation Interval Timer  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Meditation Interval Timer is a simple, offline meditation timer. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app uses and why.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not track your activity, and does not communicate with any external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your timer presets and volume settings locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The "Buy me a mint tea" button links to PayPal in your browser — this is an external link and is governed by PayPal's own privacy policy. No data is shared with PayPal from within the app.

Microphone & Camera

The app does not request access to the microphone, camera, location, contacts, or any other device sensors.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at oleinikovs.v@gmail.com
